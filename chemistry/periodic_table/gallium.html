Understanding the 4p Orbital

The atomic ground state configuration of gallium is [Ar] 3d¹⁰ 4s² 4p¹. The final valence electron resides in a dumbbell-shaped p-orbital. Highly directional along its major coordinate axis, this orbital acts like an atomic anchor seeking a partner.
